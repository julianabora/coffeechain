About

The Feminine Strength Behind Quality Coffee Production Elizabeth Malvezzi Brambila and her family, from Cafezal das Torres farm, were finalists in the Pulped Natural category of Expocacer's 2025 Champions Stage.

The farm is a true example of dedication, mastering the Pulped Natural process to deliver a clean and bright cup profile.

Certifications such as NESPRESSO, RA (Rainforest Alliance), and RCM (Cerrado Mineiro Region) reflect the rigor and responsibility of its management, with a strong focus on quality and environmental respect.
