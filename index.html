Our Value Proposition

  • These Controlled Origin Coffees come from farms within our Designation of Origin and express the unique flavor of the Cerrado Mineiro. Versatile, they are blends created to maintain consistency year after year.

    There are 4 blends, each with a sensory profile:

    • Origin: Standard of the Cerrado Mineiro Region
    • Origin Fruity: Fruity notes
    • Origin Floral: Floral notes
    • Origin Decaf: Decaffeinated option

  • Single farm coffees, with full traceability, providing detailed information such as batch, variety, process, quantity and sensory notes.

  • Unique and differentiated lots, including those destined for auction, from specific plots and exotic varieties. They stand out for their innovative processes, such as anaerobic fermentation and infused coffees, always accompanied by full traceability.
