About

Located in Guimarania/MG, Posse da Cachoeira Farm sits at 1,180 meters of altitude, with 72 hectares of coffee and 54 of eucalyptus, creating a unique microclimate for high-quality coffees.

Through Integrated Pest Management (IPM), the farm reduced pesticide use by 56%, adopting composting, cover crops, and biological control. Certified by C.A.F.E. Practices and Nespresso, it meets strict ESG standards.

The producer also takes part in Expocacer’s programs — Elas no Café, Essências, and Educampo — enhancing management and sustainable coffee production. Every achievement reflects the dedication and unity of a family guided by balance, responsibility, and love for the land.
