THE STRENGTH OF THE PRODUCER IN THE ESSENCE OF GREAT COFFEES

You are receiving 15 green coffee samples from cooperative members participating in the Essências by Expocacer project — 2025/2026 crop, an initiative that values the work of producers dedicated to quality, traceability, and sustainability.

The Essências by Expocacer project was created to transform talent into opportunity and to share with the world the authenticity of coffees from the Cerrado Mineiro region.

Now in its third edition, the project brings together 49 producers who have improved their cup scores by an average of +2.83 points over recent crops — a direct result of technical guidance and a shared commitment to excellence. More than 67% of the samples scored above 86 points, and four coffee lots reached 90 points, representing the highest expression of cooperative coffee production from the Cerrado Mineiro.

Each cup reflects the essence of a region that produces with purpose, collaboration, and identity.
